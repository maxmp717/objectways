Project Manager

Karur,India | Full-Time
Apply For this Job

The project manager role is for an experienced management position for individuals who are interested in working in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Industry. We are looking for a candidate who has experience in managing multiple projects and teams of 25+ resources is a self-starter, detailed oriented, and adaptable. The role will focus on the execution of data labeling projects for our Fortune 500 customers and will also include customer communications and cross-functional communication with Pre-sales and management. The role will be perfect for individuals who value challenge in their work.

Responsibilities

  • Project Manage 1 to many projects.
  • Manage a team of data labeling consultants and team leads.
  • Manage deliverable and project timelines.
  • Proactively provide daily reporting to management and customer.
  • Responsible for Quality assurance of projects.
  • Identify and resolve the root cause of low quality and provide feedback to respective teams on product improvements.
Desired Skills and Experience

  • Demonstrable IT Project Management experience.
  • Familiarity with reporting tools as spreadsheets.
  • Detail-oriented and can follow complex instructions.
  • Must be able to pass Background Check as we are SOC2 company.
  • Possession of excellent oral and written skills.
  • Strong sense of ownership, urgency, and drive.
  • Ability to handle ambiguity and changing priorities.
  • Familiarity with Machine Learning basics or Data labeling is a plus but not required.
  • Familiarity with AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud is a plus but not required.
  • PMP Certification is a plus.
About Us

At Objectways, our mission is to accelerate the development of Machine Learning and AI applications across multiple markets. Our focus is to deliver high-quality data labeling and human in-loop projects for Fortune 500 customers in partnership with AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Objectways is an equal opportunity employer.
