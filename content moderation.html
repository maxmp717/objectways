Content Moderation Services

Image & Text Moderation

Cost-effective image and text moderation solutions for all types of online platforms. Our trained annotators protect your platform and audience from explicit, offensive, and inappropriate visual and textual content.

Video Moderation

Let our expert team review your video to capture nuances - such as sexually suggestive behavior, nudity, violence, hateful video, and weapons in real time. We can also block or reject broken videos and flag foul videos.

Audio Moderation

With the rise in chat room startups like Clubhouse, Rodeo, Chalk and spoon - the need for audio moderation becomes as important as video moderation. Talk to our trained experts to know how we can you assist you with the audio moderation.

Why Content Moderation with Us?

Fraud

More than 2% of all user generated content is fraudulent. Protect your users against scams.

De-duplication

There are lot more duplicate content in the online marketplace. Improve your user experience by avoiding duplicate content.

Image Quality

Remove bad quality & less resolution images from your website and provide the best experience to your users.

Illegal Items

Illegal items displayed in your website will cost you bad reputation along with heafty fines from the government.

Cyberbullying

41% of Americans have been personally subjected to harassing behavior online which leads to loss in business. Protect your users from cyberbullying.

Nudity

Keep your family-friendly site from inappropriate pictures and content by rejecting them before viewed by your users.

Counterfeits

Improve your user experience by rejecting counterfeit products getting listed to your website.

Categorization

Irrelevant content or items are one of the major reasons for users to avoid your websites. Increase your web traffic by removing irrevelevant contents.
