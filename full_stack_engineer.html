Full Stack Engineer

Karur,India | Full-Time
Apply For this Job

The full stack engineer role is for an experienced Software developer who are interested in building cool product to solve complex machine learning problems. We are looking for a Sr. engineer with experience managing teams and great communication skills to ensure the success of our project and our clients, which are leading companies in AI and driving disruption in autonomous driving, robotics, financial among others.

Ideally, You'd have

  • 4 or + years of experience working as a Full Stack Engineer.
  • Communication and Team Management skills.
  • Be able, and willing, to multi-task and learn new technologies quickly.
  • Self-starter, willing to be challenged with new responsibilities, and high attention to details.
  • Ability & comfort taking architectural decisions autonomously and proactively.
  • Deep knowledge of JavasScript, Typescript, React, Node, HTML/CSS, Python and MongoDB.
Nice to have

  • Prior startup experience.
  • Strong technical (written and verbal) communication, prioritization, and time management skills.
  • Mentored and grown members of your team or been a tech lead on large projects.
